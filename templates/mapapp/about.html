About Zaza Map

Zaza Map is a community platform where cannabis enthusiasts can discover and share comfortable smoking spots.

Our Mission

To create a community-driven resource that helps people find peaceful, scenic, and safe locations to enjoy cannabis in a responsible manner.

How It Works

Click anywhere on the map to add a smoking spot you'd like to share. Add details about the location, ambiance, privacy level, and any tips for fellow enthusiasts. Rate spots you've visited to help others find the best experiences.

Community Guidelines

Please respect local regulations and be mindful of others when using and sharing locations. Our community values discretion, cleanliness, and responsible consumption.
